# Hi, I’m Maria Roszyk 👋

I’m a product engineer, artist and community builder.

I build human-centred products around family life, learning and memory. Before moving into software, I worked across contemporary art, AR/VR and community building.

I’m currently building a private, parent-controlled AI agent that helps families turn what they know about their children into relevant books, projects and learning experiences.

## What I’m building

### AI learning agent for families

A private AI agent that remembers a child’s interests, strengths and previous responses, then helps parents discover relevant audiobooks, books, projects and experiences.

The goal is not to optimise or evaluate children. It is to help parents notice what matters, act on it and build on what genuinely engages their child.

### Baby Steps

A mobile journal for parents to record children’s memories, milestones and development.

I designed and built the product independently, from product concept and UX through implementation and mobile release.

### Keeper: Regain My Love

A subscription-based relationship app with guided audio programmes, weekly check-ins, monthly dialogue tools and support for difficult moments.

I designed and built the product independently. It started acquiring paying subscribers organically, without paid marketing.

[View Keeper on the App Store](https://apps.apple.com/pl/app/keeper-regain-my-love/id6755595829)

## Previous work

My earlier work includes:

## How I work

I usually work across the full product process:

- identifying a real human problem;
- shaping the product and user experience;
- designing the interface;
- building the frontend and product logic;
- testing with users;
- shipping and iterating.

My current stack includes TypeScript, React, React Native, Expo, Node.js, PostgreSQL and AI-assisted development tools.

I care more about building a useful, coherent product than about using a particular framework.
